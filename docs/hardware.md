# Hardware

## Recommended (v1-alpha target)

- Raspberry Pi 4 (4GB recommended)
- HyperPixel 4.0 touchscreen (GPIO)
- HDMI monitor/TV
- 2x RTL-SDR dongles
  - SDR A: FM broadcast + RDS
  - SDR B: NOAA Weather Radio + SAME
- Antennas
  - FM broadcast antenna
  - VHF antenna tuned around 162.5 MHz
- Optional: powered USB hub (strongly recommended for 2 dongles)
- Speakers
  - 3.5mm analog OR HDMI audio OR Bluetooth speaker

## Notes

- 1 SDR works for either FM or NOAA, but **FM+WX override mode** requires 2 receivers.
- Keep SDRs away from noisy USB power devices where possible.
